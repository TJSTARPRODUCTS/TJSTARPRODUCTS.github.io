About TJ Star

TJ Star was born from the inspiration of my two adorable kids. During this pandemic, it was challenging to keep my little princess engaged as there wasn't much social interactions. This inspired me to create worksheets and other types of teaching resources that contain hands-on-activities for her to learn and have fun at the same time.

These worksheets did managed to keep her busy and happy. Hence, I decided to share my resources with you guys so that you can keep your young prince and princess busy while still have fun! TJ Star is about learning via hands-on-activities, inspiring and sowing the seeds in young minds to create a fondness towards education.
